# Privacy Policy for Stay Focus - AuraQuarium

Last Updated: May 2026

Stay Focus - AuraQuarium ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Information Collection
The App does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. All data, including focus session progress and virtual aquarium states, is stored **locally** on your device using browser local storage or native storage.

## 2. Information Usage
Since no personal information is collected, we do not use, sell, or share any of your data. The focus progress data remains on your device to provide the intended functionality of the app.

## 3. Data Storage and Export
All application data is stored on your device. You have the option to manually export your data as a base64-encoded string for the purpose of backup or transferring it to another device. This process is initiated solely by the user and is not automated or sent to any external servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App may use standard mobile platform features (like Android/Capacitor core plugins) which do not involve data collection by us. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the support channels provided on the Google Play Store.
